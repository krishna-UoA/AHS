About the Platform

AB Aqua Sustainable Solutions

Asterion Hydro Shield (AHS) is a cutting-edge software platform developed to revolutionise how geohazards are assessed and understood. It integrates high-resolution environmental, climatic, geological, and socioeconomic data to provide comprehensive risk intelligence.

Designed for environmental strategists, disaster risk managers, policymakers, and infrastructure planners, AHS enables users to anticipate and prepare for complex, interlinked risks before they materialise into crises.
